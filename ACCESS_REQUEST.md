# Access Request

The private Consequence Warehouse Safety engine is not included in this public
proof kit.

Private access may be considered for serious technical review, diligence,
research review, customer discovery, pilot planning, or commercial licensing
discussion.

Commercial licensing enquiries should be directed to the COO of TWO HANDS
NETWORK LTD or the company licensing contact designated by TWO HANDS NETWORK
LTD.

## Request Checklist

Before requesting access, please prepare:

- name and organisation
- contact route
- intended use
- whether the review is personal, academic, commercial, or investor diligence
- whether robot routing, safety, simulation, model training, or commercial AI
  integration is involved
- requested access level
- whether an NDA can be signed
- whether access is needed to source, private wheel, benchmark internals,
  evidence runs, or only a guided walkthrough

## Access Levels

Possible access levels:

- public proof-kit review
- guided private walkthrough
- NDA-bound technical review
- customer-run pilot
- owner-run pilot on approved sample data
- commercial licensing discussion with TWO HANDS NETWORK LTD

Private source access is not automatic. It requires written approval and may
require a signed NDA, commercial evaluation agreement, or separate written
commercial license.
